# ARV-PROOF ENTERPRISE
### Cryptographic Integrity Proof for AI Governance

**ARV-Proof Enterprise** is a sovereign cryptographic integrity layer for digital assets, datasets, models, and AI-generated outputs.

It enables organizations to prove — online or offline — that a digital asset has not been altered after origin, using portable cryptographic records, verifiable certificates, signed payloads, and dispute-ready evidence packages.

---

## 1. Problema

Organizations operating under growing AI governance and transparency obligations face a structural gap:

- dashboards do not prove integrity
- policies do not prove what existed at a specific moment
- cloud-native logs do not guarantee sovereign portability
- screenshots, exports, and ordinary records are easy to challenge
- provenance without offline verification is insufficient in adversarial or regulated contexts

As AI-generated assets, datasets, prompts, outputs, and model artifacts become operationally relevant, enterprises need a way to demonstrate cryptographic integrity beyond simple compliance documentation.

---

## 2. Solución

ARV-Proof Enterprise provides:

- cryptographic integrity proof
- portable evidence packages
- offline-verifiable certificates
- signed QR payloads
- append-only validation records
- dispute-ready artifacts
- on-prem / air-gap capable workflows
- future-ready interoperability with AI governance and evidence controls

ARV does not replace governance platforms.  
ARV strengthens them with verifiable proof.

---

## 3. Qué emite ARV

ARV-Proof Enterprise can issue:

- Validation Records
- Certificate HTML
- Certificate PDF
- QR signed payloads
- Evidence Package ZIP
- Public Verification Records
- Dispute Packages
- append-only ledger-ready entries

All outputs derive from the same canonical record.

---

## 4. Qué prueba ARV

ARV can prove:

- that a specific digital asset produced a specific hash
- that the corresponding record was sealed at a specific time
- that the record remains cryptographically consistent
- that the certificate, payload, and evidence package verify against the same canonical source
- that the proof can survive outside the platform

ARV does not claim legal truth, semantic truth, or authorship by itself.
